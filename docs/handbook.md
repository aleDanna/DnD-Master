# D&D 5th Edition Player's Handbook - Complete Summary

## Introduction
- D&D is a storytelling game in worlds of swords and sorcery
- Driven by imagination and collaborative creation
- The Dungeon Master (DM) is the game's lead storyteller and referee
- Players create adventurers and work together as a party
- **Worlds of Adventure**
  - D&D worlds exist within a vast multiverse
  - Official settings: Forgotten Realms, Dragonlance, Greyhawk, Dark Sun, Mystara, Eberron
  - Each world has its own history, cultures, monsters, and geography
- **How to Play**
  1. The DM describes the environment
  2. The players describe what they want to do
  3. The DM narrates the results of their actions
- **Game Dice**
  - d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20
  - Percentile dice (d100): two d10s
  - Notation: "3d8 + 5" = roll three d8s, add them, add 5
- **The d20**
  - Core mechanic for success/failure
  - Roll d20 + modifiers vs. target number
  - Ability checks, attack rolls, saving throws
  - Difficulty Class (DC) for checks/saves
  - Armor Class (AC) for attacks
- **Advantage and Disadvantage**
  - Advantage: roll 2d20, use higher
  - Disadvantage: roll 2d20, use lower
- **Specific Beats General**
  - Specific rules override general rules
- **Round Down**
  - Always round down fractions
- **Three Pillars of Adventure**
  - Exploration
  - Social Interaction
  - Combat

## Part 1: Creating a Character

### Chapter 1: Step-by-Step Characters

- **1. Choose a Race**
  - Grants racial traits, ability score increases
  - Note starting languages and base speed
- **2. Choose a Class**
  - Defines vocation and special talents
  - Grants proficiencies and class features
  - **Level**: starts at 1st, advances with XP
  - **Hit Points and Hit Dice**: determined by class
  - **Proficiency Bonus**: +2 at 1st level, increases as you level
- **3. Determine Ability Scores**
  - Six abilities: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma
  - Roll 4d6, drop lowest, repeat 6 times
  - Or use standard array: 15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8
  - Apply racial modifiers
  - Ability Modifiers: (Score - 10) / 2, round down
- **4. Describe Your Character**
  - Name, appearance, personality
  - Alignment, ideals, bonds, flaws
  - Background (grants features and proficiencies)
- **5. Choose Equipment**
  - Starting equipment from class and background
  - Or purchase with starting gold
  - Calculate Armor Class (AC)
  - Calculate weapon attack/damage modifiers
- **6. Come Together**
  - Form a party with other adventurers

- **Beyond 1st Level**
  - Gain XP from adventures
  - Level up: gain class features, ability score improvements, Hit Dice
  - **Tiers of Play**:
    - Tier 1 (Levels 1-4): Apprentice adventurers
    - Tier 2 (Levels 5-10): Come into their own
    - Tier 3 (Levels 11-16): High above ordinary populace
    - Tier 4 (Levels 17-20): Pinnacle of class features

### Chapter 2: Races

#### Racial Traits
- Ability Score Increase
- Age
- Alignment
- Size
- Speed
- Languages
- Subraces

#### Dwarf
- **Ability Score Increase**: Constitution +2
- **Age**: Mature same rate as humans, adult at 50, live ~350 years
- **Alignment**: Mostly lawful, tend toward good
- **Size**: Medium (4-5 feet, ~150 lbs)
- **Speed**: 25 feet (not reduced by heavy armor)
- **Darkvision**: 60 feet
- **Dwarven Resilience**: Advantage on saves vs. poison, resistance to poison damage
- **Dwarven Combat Training**: Proficient with battleaxe, handaxe, throwing hammer, warhammer
- **Tool Proficiency**: Smith's tools, brewer's supplies, or mason's tools
- **Stonecunning**: Double proficiency on History checks for stonework origin
- **Languages**: Common, Dwarvish
- **Subraces**:
  - **Hill Dwarf**: Wisdom +1, Dwarven Toughness (+1 HP per level)
  - **Mountain Dwarf**: Strength +2, proficiency with light and medium armor

#### Elf
- **Ability Score Increase**: Dexterity +2
- **Age**: Physical maturity ~same as humans, adulthood at 100, live ~750 years
- **Alignment**: Love freedom, lean toward chaos and good
- **Size**: Medium (under 5 to over 6 feet, slender)
- **Speed**: 30 feet
- **Darkvision**: 60 feet
- **Keen Senses**: Proficiency in Perception
- **Fey Ancestry**: Advantage on saves vs. charm, immune to magical sleep
- **Trance**: Meditate 4 hours instead of 8 hours sleep
- **Languages**: Common, Elvish
- **Subraces**:
  - **High Elf**: Intelligence +1, Elf Weapon Training, one wizard cantrip, extra language
  - **Wood Elf**: Wisdom +1, Elf Weapon Training, Fleet of Foot (35 ft speed), Mask of the Wild
  - **Dark Elf (Drow)**: Charisma +1, Superior Darkvision (120 ft), Sunlight Sensitivity, Drow Magic, Drow Weapon Training

#### Halfling
- **Ability Score Increase**: Dexterity +2
- **Age**: Adult at 20, live into middle of second century
- **Alignment**: Mostly lawful good
- **Size**: Small (~3 feet, ~40 lbs)
- **Speed**: 25 feet
- **Lucky**: Reroll 1s on attack rolls, ability checks, saving throws
- **Brave**: Advantage on saves vs. being frightened
- **Halfling Nimbleness**: Move through space of larger creatures
- **Languages**: Common, Halfling
- **Subraces**:
  - **Lightfoot**: Charisma +1, Naturally Stealthy (hide behind larger creatures)
  - **Stout**: Constitution +1, Stout Resilience (advantage on poison saves, resistance to poison)

#### Human
- **Ability Score Increase**: All abilities +1
- **Age**: Adult in late teens, live less than a century
- **Alignment**: No particular tendency
- **Size**: Medium (5-6+ feet)
- **Speed**: 30 feet
- **Languages**: Common plus one extra
- **Human Ethnicities**: Calishite, Chondathan, Damaran, Illuskan, Mulan, Rashemi, Shou, Tethyrian, Turami
- **Variant Human**: Two different abilities +1, one skill proficiency, one feat

#### Dragonborn
- **Ability Score Increase**: Strength +2, Charisma +1
- **Age**: Walk hours after hatching, adult by 15, live to ~80
- **Alignment**: Tend to extremes, mostly good
- **Size**: Medium (over 6 feet, ~250 lbs)
- **Speed**: 30 feet
- **Draconic Ancestry**: Choose dragon type (determines breath weapon and resistance)
  - Black (Acid, 5x30 ft line), Blue (Lightning, 5x30 ft line), Brass (Fire, 5x30 ft line)
  - Bronze (Lightning, 5x30 ft line), Copper (Acid, 5x30 ft line), Gold (Fire, 15 ft cone)
  - Green (Poison, 15 ft cone), Red (Fire, 15 ft cone), Silver (Cold, 15 ft cone), White (Cold, 15 ft cone)
- **Breath Weapon**: 2d6 damage (increases at 6th, 11th, 16th level), DC = 8 + Con mod + proficiency
- **Damage Resistance**: Resistance to ancestry damage type
- **Languages**: Common, Draconic

#### Gnome
- **Ability Score Increase**: Intelligence +2
- **Age**: Mature same rate as humans, adult at 40, live 350-500 years
- **Alignment**: Good, often chaotic
- **Size**: Small (3-4 feet, ~40 lbs)
- **Speed**: 25 feet
- **Darkvision**: 60 feet
- **Gnome Cunning**: Advantage on Int, Wis, Cha saves vs. magic
- **Languages**: Common, Gnomish
- **Subraces**:
  - **Forest Gnome**: Dexterity +1, Natural Illusionist (minor illusion cantrip), Speak with Small Beasts
  - **Rock Gnome**: Constitution +1, Artificer's Lore, Tinker (create clockwork devices)

#### Half-Elf
- **Ability Score Increase**: Charisma +2, two other abilities +1
- **Age**: Mature same rate as humans, adult at 20, live 180+ years
- **Alignment**: Chaotic bent
- **Size**: Medium (5-6 feet)
- **Speed**: 30 feet
- **Darkvision**: 60 feet
- **Fey Ancestry**: Advantage on saves vs. charm, immune to magical sleep
- **Skill Versatility**: Proficiency in two skills of choice
- **Languages**: Common, Elvish, one extra

#### Half-Orc
- **Ability Score Increase**: Strength +2, Constitution +1
- **Age**: Mature faster, adult at 14, rarely live past 75
- **Alignment**: Tend toward chaos
- **Size**: Medium (5-7 feet)
- **Speed**: 30 feet
- **Darkvision**: 60 feet
- **Menacing**: Proficiency in Intimidation
- **Relentless Endurance**: Drop to 1 HP instead of 0 (once per long rest)
- **Savage Attacks**: Extra damage die on critical hits with melee weapons
- **Languages**: Common, Orc

#### Tiefling
- **Ability Score Increase**: Intelligence +1, Charisma +2
- **Age**: Mature same rate as humans, live slightly longer
- **Alignment**: Often chaotic
- **Size**: Medium (same as humans)
- **Speed**: 30 feet
- **Darkvision**: 60 feet
- **Hellish Resistance**: Resistance to fire damage
- **Infernal Legacy**: Thaumaturgy cantrip, hellish rebuke (3rd level), darkness (5th level)
- **Languages**: Common, Infernal

### Chapter 3: Classes

#### Barbarian
- **Hit Die**: d12
- **Primary Ability**: Strength
- **Saves**: Strength, Constitution
- **Proficiencies**: Light/medium armor, shields, simple/martial weapons
- **Class Features**:
  - **Rage**: Bonus action, advantage on Str checks/saves, bonus rage damage, resistance to physical damage
  - **Unarmored Defense**: AC = 10 + Dex mod + Con mod
  - **Reckless Attack** (2nd): Advantage on melee attacks, attacks against you have advantage
  - **Danger Sense** (2nd): Advantage on Dex saves vs. visible effects
  - **Primal Path** (3rd): Choose subclass
  - **Extra Attack** (5th): Two attacks per Attack action
  - **Fast Movement** (5th): +10 ft speed when not in heavy armor
  - **Feral Instinct** (7th): Advantage on initiative
  - **Brutal Critical** (9th, 13th, 17th): Extra damage dice on crits
  - **Relentless Rage** (11th): Con save to stay at 1 HP
  - **Persistent Rage** (15th): Rage doesn't end early
  - **Indomitable Might** (18th): Use Str score if total is lower
  - **Primal Champion** (20th): Str and Con +4
- **Primal Paths**:
  - **Path of the Berserker**: Frenzy, Mindless Rage, Intimidating Presence, Retaliation
  - **Path of the Totem Warrior**: Totem Spirit (Bear/Eagle/Wolf), Aspect of the Beast, Spirit Walker, Totemic Attunement

#### Bard
- **Hit Die**: d8
- **Primary Ability**: Charisma
- **Saves**: Dexterity, Charisma
- **Proficiencies**: Light armor, simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords, three musical instruments
- **Spellcasting**: Charisma-based, known spells
- **Class Features**:
  - **Bardic Inspiration**: Bonus die (d6→d12) to ability checks, attacks, or saves
  - **Jack of All Trades** (2nd): +half proficiency to non-proficient checks
  - **Song of Rest** (2nd): Extra healing on short rest (d6→d12)
  - **Bard College** (3rd): Choose subclass
  - **Expertise** (3rd, 10th): Double proficiency in two skills
  - **Font of Inspiration** (5th): Regain Bardic Inspiration on short rest
  - **Countercharm** (6th): Advantage on saves vs. charm/fear for allies
  - **Magical Secrets** (10th, 14th, 18th): Learn spells from any class
  - **Superior Inspiration** (20th): Regain one Bardic Inspiration if none left
- **Bard Colleges**:
  - **College of Lore**: Bonus Proficiencies, Cutting Words, Additional Magical Secrets, Peerless Skill
  - **College of Valor**: Bonus Proficiencies (armor/weapons), Combat Inspiration, Extra Attack, Battle Magic

#### Cleric
- **Hit Die**: d8
- **Primary Ability**: Wisdom
- **Saves**: Wisdom, Charisma
- **Proficiencies**: Light/medium armor, shields, simple weapons
- **Spellcasting**: Wisdom-based, prepared spells
- **Class Features**:
  - **Divine Domain** (1st): Choose subclass, grants domain spells
  - **Channel Divinity** (2nd): Turn Undead + domain options
  - **Destroy Undead** (5th): Destroyed instead of turned (CR threshold increases)
  - **Divine Intervention** (10th): Appeal to deity for aid
- **Divine Domains**:
  - **Knowledge**: Blessings of Knowledge, Channel Divinity (Knowledge of Ages, Read Thoughts), Potent Spellcasting, Visions of the Past
  - **Life**: Bonus Proficiency, Disciple of Life, Channel Divinity (Preserve Life), Blessed Healer, Divine Strike, Supreme Healing
  - **Light**: Bonus Cantrip (light), Warding Flare, Channel Divinity (Radiance of the Dawn), Improved Flare, Potent Spellcasting, Corona of Light
  - **Nature**: Acolyte of Nature, Bonus Proficiency, Channel Divinity (Charm Animals and Plants), Dampen Elements, Divine Strike, Master of Nature
  - **Tempest**: Bonus Proficiencies, Wrath of the Storm, Channel Divinity (Destructive Wrath), Thunderbolt Strike, Divine Strike, Stormborn
  - **Trickery**: Blessing of the Trickster, Channel Divinity (Invoke Duplicity, Cloak of Shadows), Divine Strike, Improved Duplicity
  - **War**: Bonus Proficiencies, War Priest, Channel Divinity (Guided Strike, War God's Blessing), Divine Strike, Avatar of Battle

#### Druid
- **Hit Die**: d8
- **Primary Ability**: Wisdom
- **Saves**: Intelligence, Wisdom
- **Proficiencies**: Light/medium armor (non-metal), shields (non-metal), clubs, daggers, darts, javelins, maces, quarterstaffs, scimitars, sickles, slings, spears, herbalism kit
- **Spellcasting**: Wisdom-based, prepared spells
- **Class Features**:
  - **Druidic** (1st): Secret language
  - **Wild Shape** (2nd): Transform into beasts (CR and movement limits improve with level)
  - **Druid Circle** (2nd): Choose subclass
  - **Land's Stride** (6th): Move through nonmagical difficult terrain
  - **Nature's Ward** (10th): Immune to poison, disease, charm/frighten by elementals/fey
  - **Nature's Sanctuary** (14th): Beasts and plants hesitate to attack
  - **Beast Spells** (18th): Cast spells in Wild Shape
  - **Archdruid** (20th): Unlimited Wild Shape
- **Druid Circles**:
  - **Circle of the Land**: Bonus cantrip, Natural Recovery, Circle Spells (by terrain), Land's Stride, Nature's Ward, Nature's Sanctuary
  - **Circle of the Moon**: Combat Wild Shape, Circle Forms (higher CR), Primal Strike, Elemental Wild Shape, Thousand Forms

#### Fighter
- **Hit Die**: d10
- **Primary Ability**: Strength or Dexterity
- **Saves**: Strength, Constitution
- **Proficiencies**: All armor, shields, simple/martial weapons
- **Class Features**:
  - **Fighting Style** (1st): Archery, Defense, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting, Protection, Two-Weapon Fighting
  - **Second Wind** (1st): Heal 1d10 + level as bonus action
  - **Action Surge** (2nd): Extra action once per rest
  - **Martial Archetype** (3rd): Choose subclass
  - **Extra Attack** (5th, 11th, 20th): Multiple attacks (up to 4)
  - **Indomitable** (9th, 13th, 17th): Reroll failed save
- **Martial Archetypes**:
  - **Champion**: Improved Critical, Remarkable Athlete, Additional Fighting Style, Superior Critical, Survivor
  - **Battle Master**: Combat Superiority (maneuvers + superiority dice), Student of War, Know Your Enemy, Improved Combat Superiority, Relentless
  - **Eldritch Knight**: Spellcasting (wizard spells), Weapon Bond, War Magic, Eldritch Strike, Arcane Charge, Improved War Magic

#### Monk
- **Hit Die**: d8
- **Primary Ability**: Dexterity and Wisdom
- **Saves**: Strength, Dexterity
- **Proficiencies**: Simple weapons, shortswords
- **Class Features**:
  - **Unarmored Defense**: AC = 10 + Dex mod + Wis mod
  - **Martial Arts**: Use Dex for monk weapons, martial arts die (d4→d10), bonus unarmed strike
  - **Ki** (2nd): Ki points = level, fuel special abilities
    - Flurry of Blows, Patient Defense, Step of the Wind
  - **Unarmored Movement** (2nd): +speed bonus (10→30 ft)
  - **Monastic Tradition** (3rd): Choose subclass
  - **Deflect Missiles** (3rd): Reduce ranged weapon damage
  - **Slow Fall** (4th): Reduce falling damage
  - **Extra Attack** (5th)
  - **Stunning Strike** (5th): Spend ki to stun on hit
  - **Ki-Empowered Strikes** (6th): Unarmed strikes are magical
  - **Evasion** (7th): No damage on successful Dex save, half on fail
  - **Stillness of Mind** (7th): End charm/frighten effects
  - **Purity of Body** (10th): Immune to disease and poison
  - **Tongue of the Sun and Moon** (13th): Understand all spoken languages
  - **Diamond Soul** (14th): Proficiency in all saves
  - **Timeless Body** (15th): No aging penalties
  - **Empty Body** (18th): Invisible, resistance to all damage
  - **Perfect Self** (20th): Regain ki on initiative if empty
- **Monastic Traditions**:
  - **Way of the Open Hand**: Open Hand Technique, Wholeness of Body, Tranquility, Quivering Palm
  - **Way of Shadow**: Shadow Arts, Shadow Step, Cloak of Shadows, Opportunist
  - **Way of the Four Elements**: Elemental Disciplines (casting elemental spells with ki)

#### Paladin
- **Hit Die**: d10
- **Primary Ability**: Strength and Charisma
- **Saves**: Wisdom, Charisma
- **Proficiencies**: All armor, shields, simple/martial weapons
- **Spellcasting** (2nd): Charisma-based, prepared spells
- **Class Features**:
  - **Divine Sense** (1st): Detect celestials, fiends, undead
  - **Lay on Hands** (1st): Healing pool = 5 x level
  - **Fighting Style** (2nd): Defense, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting, Protection
  - **Divine Smite** (2nd): Extra radiant damage on hit (spell slot)
  - **Divine Health** (3rd): Immune to disease
  - **Sacred Oath** (3rd): Choose subclass, grants oath spells and Channel Divinity
  - **Extra Attack** (5th)
  - **Aura of Protection** (6th): +Cha mod to saves for nearby allies
  - **Aura of Courage** (10th): Immune to fear, allies too while nearby
  - **Improved Divine Smite** (11th): +1d8 radiant on all melee hits
  - **Cleansing Touch** (14th): End spells on self or ally
- **Sacred Oaths**:
  - **Oath of Devotion**: Sacred Weapon, Turn the Unholy, Aura of Devotion, Purity of Spirit, Holy Nimbus
  - **Oath of the Ancients**: Nature's Wrath, Turn the Faithless, Aura of Warding, Undying Sentinel, Elder Champion
  - **Oath of Vengeance**: Abjure Enemy, Vow of Enmity, Relentless Avenger, Soul of Vengeance, Avenging Angel

#### Ranger
- **Hit Die**: d10
- **Primary Ability**: Dexterity and Wisdom
- **Saves**: Strength, Dexterity
- **Proficiencies**: Light/medium armor, shields, simple/martial weapons
- **Spellcasting** (2nd): Wisdom-based, known spells
- **Class Features**:
  - **Favored Enemy** (1st): Advantage on tracking, Intelligence checks
  - **Natural Explorer** (1st): Terrain expertise
  - **Fighting Style** (2nd): Archery, Defense, Dueling, Two-Weapon Fighting
  - **Ranger Archetype** (3rd): Choose subclass
  - **Primeval Awareness** (3rd): Sense aberrations, celestials, dragons, elementals, fey, fiends, undead
  - **Extra Attack** (5th)
  - **Land's Stride** (8th): Move through nonmagical difficult terrain
  - **Hide in Plain Sight** (10th): Camouflage bonus to Stealth
  - **Vanish** (14th): Hide as bonus action, can't be tracked
  - **Feral Senses** (18th): Fight invisible creatures
  - **Foe Slayer** (20th): +Wis mod to attack or damage vs. favored enemy
- **Ranger Archetypes**:
  - **Hunter**: Hunter's Prey, Defensive Tactics, Multiattack, Superior Hunter's Defense
  - **Beast Master**: Ranger's Companion, Exceptional Training, Bestial Fury, Share Spells

#### Rogue
- **Hit Die**: d8
- **Primary Ability**: Dexterity
- **Saves**: Dexterity, Intelligence
- **Proficiencies**: Light armor, simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords, thieves' tools
- **Class Features**:
  - **Expertise** (1st, 6th): Double proficiency in chosen skills
  - **Sneak Attack** (1st): Extra damage (1d6→10d6) with advantage or ally nearby
  - **Thieves' Cant** (1st): Secret language
  - **Cunning Action** (2nd): Bonus action Dash, Disengage, or Hide
  - **Roguish Archetype** (3rd): Choose subclass
  - **Uncanny Dodge** (5th): Halve damage from visible attacker
  - **Evasion** (7th): No damage on Dex save success
  - **Reliable Talent** (11th): Minimum 10 on proficient skill checks
  - **Blindsense** (14th): Locate hidden creatures within 10 ft
  - **Slippery Mind** (15th): Proficiency in Wisdom saves
  - **Elusive** (18th): No advantage on attacks against you
  - **Stroke of Luck** (20th): Turn miss into hit or 20 on ability check
- **Roguish Archetypes**:
  - **Thief**: Fast Hands, Second-Story Work, Supreme Sneak, Use Magic Device, Thief's Reflexes
  - **Assassin**: Bonus Proficiencies, Assassinate, Infiltration Expertise, Impostor, Death Strike
  - **Arcane Trickster**: Spellcasting (wizard spells), Mage Hand Legerdemain, Magical Ambush, Versatile Trickster, Spell Thief

#### Sorcerer
- **Hit Die**: d6
- **Primary Ability**: Charisma
- **Saves**: Constitution, Charisma
- **Proficiencies**: Daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows
- **Spellcasting**: Charisma-based, known spells
- **Class Features**:
  - **Sorcerous Origin** (1st): Choose subclass
  - **Font of Magic** (2nd): Sorcery points, convert to/from spell slots
  - **Metamagic** (3rd): Modify spells (Careful, Distant, Empowered, Extended, Heightened, Quickened, Subtle, Twinned)
  - **Sorcerous Restoration** (20th): Regain 4 sorcery points on short rest
- **Sorcerous Origins**:
  - **Draconic Bloodline**: Dragon Ancestor, Draconic Resilience (+1 HP/level, AC 13 + Dex), Elemental Affinity, Dragon Wings, Draconic Presence
  - **Wild Magic**: Wild Magic Surge, Tides of Chaos, Bend Luck, Controlled Chaos, Spell Bombardment

#### Warlock
- **Hit Die**: d8
- **Primary Ability**: Charisma
- **Saves**: Wisdom, Charisma
- **Proficiencies**: Light armor, simple weapons
- **Pact Magic**: Short-rest spell slots, all spells at highest level
- **Class Features**:
  - **Otherworldly Patron** (1st): Choose subclass
  - **Eldritch Invocations** (2nd): Special abilities and spell enhancements
  - **Pact Boon** (3rd): Pact of the Chain (familiar), Pact of the Blade (weapon), Pact of the Tome (cantrips)
  - **Mystic Arcanum** (11th-17th): One casting per day of 6th-9th level spells
  - **Eldritch Master** (20th): Regain all spell slots by supplicating patron
- **Otherworldly Patrons**:
  - **The Archfey**: Fey Presence, Misty Escape, Beguiling Defenses, Dark Delirium
  - **The Fiend**: Dark One's Blessing, Dark One's Own Luck, Fiendish Resilience, Hurl Through Hell
  - **The Great Old One**: Awakened Mind, Entropic Ward, Thought Shield, Create Thrall

#### Wizard

- **Primary Ability**: Intelligence
- **Saves**: Intelligence, Wisdom
- **Proficiencies**: Daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows
- **Spellcasting**: Intelligence-based, spellbook (prepared spells)
- **Class Features**:
  - **Arcane Recovery** (1st): Recover spell slots on short rest
  - **Arcane Tradition** (2nd): Choose subclass
  - **Spell Mastery** (18th): Cast 1st and 2nd level spells at will
  - **Signature Spells** (20th): Two 3rd level spells always prepared, free casting
- **Arcane Traditions**:
  - **School of Abjuration**: Abjuration Savant, Arcane Ward, Projected Ward, Improved Abjuration, Spell Resistance
  - **School of Conjuration**: Conjuration Savant, Minor Conjuration, Benign Transposition, Focused Conjuration, Durable Summons
  - **School of Divination**: Divination Savant, Portent, Expert Divination, The Third Eye, Greater Portent
  - **School of Enchantment**: Enchantment Savant, Hypnotic Gaze, Instinctive Charm, Split Enchantment, Alter Memories
  - **School of Evocation**: Evocation Savant, Sculpt Spells, Potent Cantrip, Empowered Evocation, Overchannel
  - **School of Illusion**: Illusion Savant, Improved Minor Illusion, Malleable Illusions, Illusory Self, Illusory Reality
  - **School of Necromancy**: Necromancy Savant, Grim Harvest, Undead Thralls, Inured to Undeath, Command Undead
  - **School of Transmutation**: Transmutation Savant, Minor Alchemy, Transmuter's Stone, Shapechanger, Master Transmuter

### Chapter 4: Personality and Background

#### Character Details
- **Name**: Varies by race and culture
- **Sex**: Choose male or female (or other)
- **Height and Weight**: Use racial norms or randomize
- **Physical Characteristics**: Eyes, hair, skin, distinguishing marks

#### Alignment
- Two axes: Law vs. Chaos, Good vs. Evil
- **Lawful Good**: Honor and compassion
- **Neutral Good**: Best for helping others
- **Chaotic Good**: Freedom and kindness
- **Lawful Neutral**: Order and tradition
- **True Neutral**: Balance or neutrality
- **Chaotic Neutral**: Personal freedom above all
- **Lawful Evil**: Methodical and organized
- **Neutral Evil**: Self-interest without qualms
- **Chaotic Evil**: Violence and greed

#### Languages
- **Standard Languages**: Common, Dwarvish, Elvish, Giant, Gnomish, Goblin, Halfling, Orc
- **Exotic Languages**: Abyssal, Celestial, Draconic, Deep Speech, Infernal, Primordial, Sylvan, Undercommon

#### Personality Characteristics
- **Personality Traits**: Two distinctive features
- **Ideals**: Driving motivation
- **Bonds**: Connections to people, places, things
- **Flaws**: Weaknesses that can be exploited

#### Inspiration
- Awarded by DM for good roleplaying
- Spend to gain advantage on one roll
- Can give to another player

#### Backgrounds
- Grant proficiencies, languages, equipment, and features

- **Acolyte**: Temple servant
  - Skills: Insight, Religion
  - Languages: Two of choice
  - Feature: Shelter of the Faithful

- **Charlatan**: Con artist
  - Skills: Deception, Sleight of Hand
  - Tools: Disguise kit, forgery kit
  - Feature: False Identity

- **Criminal/Spy**: Lawbreaker or spy
  - Skills: Deception, Stealth
  - Tools: Gaming set, thieves' tools
  - Feature: Criminal Contact

- **Entertainer/Gladiator**: Performer or arena fighter
  - Skills: Acrobatics, Performance
  - Tools: Disguise kit, musical instrument
  - Feature: By Popular Demand

- **Folk Hero**: Champion of the common people
  - Skills: Animal Handling, Survival
  - Tools: Artisan's tools, vehicles (land)
  - Feature: Rustic Hospitality

- **Guild Artisan/Merchant**: Skilled tradesperson
  - Skills: Insight, Persuasion
  - Tools: Artisan's tools
  - Languages: One of choice
  - Feature: Guild Membership

- **Hermit**: Isolated contemplative
  - Skills: Medicine, Religion
  - Tools: Herbalism kit
  - Languages: One of choice
  - Feature: Discovery

- **Noble/Knight**: Aristocrat or knight
  - Skills: History, Persuasion
  - Tools: Gaming set

## Part 2: Playing the Game

### Chapter 7: Using Ability Scores

#### Ability Scores and Modifiers
- Score ranges typically 1-20 (monsters up to 30)
- Modifier = (Score - 10) / 2, round down

#### Advantage and Disadvantage
- Roll 2d20, take higher (advantage) or lower (disadvantage)
- Multiple sources don't stack
- Advantage and disadvantage cancel out

#### Proficiency Bonus
- Ranges from +2 (level 1) to +6 (level 17+)
- Added to: attack rolls, ability checks, saving throws, spell save DCs
- Can only be added once to any roll

#### Ability Checks
- Roll d20 + ability modifier + proficiency (if applicable) vs. DC
- Typical DCs: Very Easy (5), Easy (10), Medium (15), Hard (20), Very Hard (25), Nearly Impossible (30)

#### Skills
- **Strength**: Athletics
- **Dexterity**: Acrobatics, Sleight of Hand, Stealth
- **Intelligence**: Arcana, History, Investigation, Nature, Religion
- **Wisdom**: Animal Handling, Insight, Medicine, Perception, Survival
- **Charisma**: Deception, Intimidation, Performance, Persuasion

#### Contests
- Both participants roll ability checks
- Highest total wins, ties go to neither

#### Passive Checks
- 10 + all modifiers (add/subtract 5 for advantage/disadvantage)
- Used for repeated tasks or DM's hidden checks

#### Working Together
- One character can Help, granting advantage
- Must be able to attempt the task alone

#### Group Checks
- Everyone rolls; if half succeed, group succeeds

#### Using Each Ability
- **Strength**: Lifting, carrying, attacking with melee, Athletics
- **Dexterity**: Agility, Stealth, attacking with finesse/ranged, AC, Initiative
- **Constitution**: Health, stamina, Hit Points, concentration saves
- **Intelligence**: Reasoning, memory, Arcana, History, Investigation, Nature, Religion
- **Wisdom**: Perception, intuition, Animal Handling, Insight, Medicine, Survival
- **Charisma**: Social interaction, Deception, Intimidation, Performance, Persuasion

#### Saving Throws
- Roll d20 + ability modifier + proficiency (if proficient) vs. DC
- Resisting spells, traps, poison, etc.

### Chapter 8: Adventuring

#### Time
- Tracked in rounds (6 seconds), minutes, hours, days as appropriate

#### Movement
- **Speed**: Distance in feet moved in one round
- **Travel Pace**:
  - Fast: 400 ft/minute, 4 miles/hour, 30 miles/day (-5 passive Perception)
  - Normal: 300 ft/minute, 3 miles/hour, 24 miles/day
  - Slow: 200 ft/minute, 2 miles/hour, 18 miles/day (can use Stealth)
- **Difficult Terrain**: Half speed
- **Climbing, Swimming, Crawling**: Costs extra movement
- **Jumping**:
  - Long jump: Strength score feet (with running start)
  - High jump: 3 + Strength modifier feet (with running start)
- **Forced March**: Constitution save each hour past 8 or gain exhaustion

#### Activity While Traveling
- Marching order
- Stealth (slow pace)
- Noticing threats (passive Perception)
- Navigation, mapping, tracking, foraging

#### The Environment
- **Falling**: 1d6 damage per 10 feet (max 20d6)
- **Suffocating**: 1 + Con modifier minutes, then rounds equal to Con modifier
- **Vision and Light**:
  - Bright light: Normal vision
  - Dim light: Lightly obscured, disadvantage on Perception
  - Darkness: Heavily obscured, effectively blind
  - Darkvision: See in darkness as dim light
  - Blindsight: Perceive without sight
  - Truesight: See through magical darkness, invisibility, illusions
- **Food and Water**:
  - Need 1 lb food and 1 gallon water per day
  - Exhaustion from deprivation

#### Social Interaction
- Roleplaying (descriptive or active approach)
- NPC attitudes: Friendly, Indifferent, Hostile
- Ability checks for persuasion, deception, intimidation

#### Resting
- **Short Rest**: 1+ hours, spend Hit Dice to heal
- **Long Rest**: 8+ hours, regain all HP and half Hit Dice, regain spell slots

#### Between Adventures
- **Lifestyle Expenses**: Cost of living between adventures
- **Downtime Activities**:
  - Crafting: 5 gp progress per day
  - Practicing a Profession: Maintain lifestyle
  - Recuperating: Recover from debilitations
  - Researching: Gain information
  - Training: Learn language or tool (250 days, 1 gp/day)

### Chapter 9: Combat

#### The Order of Combat
1. Determine surprise
2. Establish positions
3. Roll initiative
4. Take turns in initiative order
5. Begin next round when everyone has acted

#### Surprise
- Stealth vs. passive Perception
- Surprised creatures can't move or act on first turn

#### Initiative
- Dexterity check at start of combat
- Determines turn order

#### Your Turn
- **Move**: Up to your speed
- **Action**: Attack, Cast a Spell, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Help, Hide, Ready, Search, Use an Object
- **Bonus Action**: Only if a feature grants one
- **Free Object Interaction**: Draw/sheathe weapon, open door, etc.
- **Reaction**: One per round (opportunity attack, etc.)

#### Movement
- Can break up movement with actions
- **Difficult Terrain**: Costs 2 feet per foot
- **Being Prone**: Standing costs half speed, crawling costs double
- **Flying**: Fall if knocked prone or speed reduced to 0

#### Actions in Combat
- **Attack**: Make one melee or ranged attack
- **Cast a Spell**: Cast with casting time of 1 action
- **Dash**: Gain extra movement equal to speed
- **Disengage**: Movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks
- **Dodge**: Attacks against you have disadvantage, advantage on Dex saves
- **Help**: Grant advantage to an ally's check or attack
- **Hide**: Make Stealth check to become hidden
- **Ready**: Prepare an action to trigger later (uses reaction)
- **Search**: Make Perception or Investigation check
- **Use an Object**: Interact with an object requiring action

#### Making an Attack
1. Choose target in range
2. Determine modifiers
3. Resolve: Roll d20 + modifiers vs. AC
- **Critical Hit**: Natural 20, roll extra damage dice
- **Critical Miss**: Natural 1, always misses

#### Cover
- Half Cover: +2 AC and Dex saves
- Three-quarters Cover: +5 AC and Dex saves
- Total Cover: Can't be targeted

#### Damage and Healing
- **Damage Types**: Acid, Bludgeoning, Cold, Fire, Force, Lightning, Necrotic, Piercing, Poison, Psychic, Radiant, Slashing, Thunder
- **Resistance**: Half damage
- **Vulnerability**: Double damage
- **Healing**: Regain hit points

#### Dropping to 0 Hit Points
- **Instant Death**: Remaining damage equals or exceeds HP maximum
- **Falling Unconscious**: 0 HP but not dead
- **Death Saving Throws**: d20 each turn at 0 HP
  - 10+ success, 3 successes = stable
  - Below 10 failure, 3 failures = death
  - Natural 1 = 2 failures
  - Natural 20 = regain 1 HP
- **Stabilizing**: DC 10 Wisdom (Medicine) check
- **Knocking Out**: Choose to incapacitate instead of kill on melee hit

#### Temporary Hit Points
- Buffer against damage
- Don't stack, choose which to keep
- Not actual HP, can't be healed

#### Mounted Combat
- Mount moves on your initiative
- Controlled mount: Dash, Disengage, Dodge only
- Independent mount: Uses own actions

#### Underwater Combat
- Disadvantage on melee attacks without swim speed (except dagger, javelin, shortsword, spear, trident)
- Ranged attacks miss beyond normal range, disadvantage within
- Resistance to fire damage

## Part 3: Magic

### Chapter 10: Spellcasting

#### What Is a Spell?
- Discrete magical effect shaped from magical energies
- Can heal, harm, create, alter, impose conditions

#### Spell Level
- 0-9, indicates power
- Cantrips are level 0
- Character level ≠ spell level

#### Known and Prepared Spells
- Some classes know fixed spells (bard, sorcerer)
- Others prepare from a list (cleric, druid, wizard)

#### Spell Slots
- Expended to cast spells
- Regained on long rest (short rest for warlock)
- Can cast lower-level spell with higher slot for increased effect

#### Casting at Higher Level
- Some spells gain power when cast with higher slot

#### Cantrips
- Cast at will, no slot needed

#### Rituals
- Cast without slot, takes 10 extra minutes

#### Casting a Spell
- **Casting Time**: Action, bonus action, reaction, or longer
- **Range**: Distance to target
- **Components**:
  - Verbal (V): Spoken words
  - Somatic (S): Hand gestures
  - Material (M): Physical components (focus can substitute unless consumed or has cost)
- **Duration**: Instantaneous, concentration, or fixed time
- **Concentration**: Broken by casting another concentration spell, taking damage (Con save DC 10 or half damage), or incapacitation

#### Targets
- Creatures, objects, or points in space
- Need clear path (no total cover)

#### Areas of Effect
- **Cone**: Width equals distance from origin
- **Cube**: Point of origin on a face
- **Cylinder**: Circle base, vertical extent
- **Line**: Straight path
- **Sphere**: Expands from center point

#### Saving Throws
- DC = 8 + proficiency + spellcasting ability modifier
- Targets roll to resist or reduce effects

#### Attack Rolls
- Spell attack = d20 + proficiency + spellcasting ability modifier
- Disadvantage within 5 feet of hostile creature

#### Schools of Magic
- **Abjuration**: Protective spells
- **Conjuration**: Transport objects/creatures
- **Divination**: Reveal information
- **Enchantment**: Affect minds
- **Evocation**: Manipulate energy (damage, healing)
- **Illusion**: Deceive senses
- **Necromancy**: Manipulate life and death
- **Transmutation**: Change properties

## Appendices

### Appendix A: Conditions
- **Blinded**: Can't see, auto-fail sight-based checks, attacks have disadvantage, attacks against have advantage
- **Charmed**: Can't attack charmer, charmer has advantage on social checks
- **Deafened**: Can't hear, auto-fail hearing-based checks
- **Exhaustion**: 6 levels, cumulative penalties (disadvantage, half speed, 0 HP, death)
- **Frightened**: Disadvantage on checks/attacks while source in sight, can't move closer
- **Grappled**: Speed 0, ends if grappler incapacitated or moved out of reach
- **Incapacitated**: Can't take actions or reactions
- **Invisible**: Heavily obscured for hiding, attacks have advantage, attacks against have disadvantage
- **Paralyzed**: Incapacitated, can't move/speak, auto-fail Str/Dex saves, attacks have advantage, melee hits are crits
- **Petrified**: Transformed to stone, weight x10, incapacitated, unaware, attacks have advantage, auto-fail Str/Dex saves, resistance to all damage, immune to poison/disease
- **Poisoned**: Disadvantage on attacks and ability checks
- **Prone**: Crawl only, disadvantage on attacks, melee attacks have advantage, ranged attacks have disadvantage
- **Restrained**: Speed 0, attacks have disadvantage, attacks against have advantage, disadvantage on Dex saves
- **Stunned**: Incapacitated, can't move, speak falteringly, auto-fail Str/Dex saves, attacks have advantage
- **Unconscious**: Incapacitated, can't move/speak, unaware, drop what held, fall prone, auto-fail Str/Dex saves, attacks have advantage, melee hits are crits
